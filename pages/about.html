About me

I'm a developer and automation specialist with a strong interest in both the technical and strategic sides of software delivery. I started coding in college, exploring Python, Assembly, and Java, then moved into web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Now I work on building reliable systems: automating tests with Cypress, managing CI/CD with Jenkins and GitLab and automating processes as much as possible to reduce friction for teams. I've worked in projects using various languages and frameworks, as I try to not be language-dependent but to be as agnostic as possible.

On a personal token, I love nature and gardening, and I teach martial arts. Feel free to contact me in the links provided below!
